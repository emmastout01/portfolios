Split It Up!

This application was created as an individual project at Prime Digital Academy. Split it Up! is a cost-sharing application that aims to provide housemates with an easy way to track and split up communal house expenses. It allows users to join a house or create an existing house and view a cost dashboard (including a "who still owes what?" table) for their house, as well as a table of all transactions for the month. Users can add, edit, and delete transactions.

Technologies Used: AngularJS, Express, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Angular Material, Passport, Moment.js, FileStack API

Andrew Residence Scheduling App

This scheduling app was created as a group project at Prime Digital Academy. Our client was Andrew Residence, a mental health facility in Minneapolis. We developed an application in which supervisors and on-call staff at Andrew Residence can communicate more efficiently about scheduling shifts. Supervisors can add and edit shifts, and on-call staff can pick up open shifts. Supervisors confirm pending shifts.

Technologies Used: AngularJS, Express, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Nodemailer, Passport, Moment.js, Plivo API, Angular Material

Totally Radish

Totally Radish won 2nd place at Hack the Gap 2018, an all-women weekend-long hack-a-thon. It was designed and developed at Hack the Gap. It is designed to simplify the process of keeping to a strict diet such as veganism. In the app, users can upload a photo of a packaged food product from their device. Upon clicking 'upload', the app will tell the user if the product is vegan. If the product is non-vegan, the app will provide a list of the non-vegan ingredients and their descriptions.

Technologies Used: AngularJS, Express, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap, Passport, NutritionIX API, OCR API
